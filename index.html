About Us

HackHSN 2019 is the first hackathon hosted by West Windsor-Plainsboro High School North.

Event Details

Date: September 21, 2019

Time: 8am - 8pm

Location: West Windsor-Plainsboro High School North: 90 Grovers Mill Rd, Plainsboro Township, NJ 08536

Who Can Attend: Any high school or college student with a passion for computer science. Absolutely no prior programming experience is required.

Cost to Attendees: This event, including food, swag, and prizes, is entirely free to attend.

FAQ

What do I need to bring?

Be sure to bring a computer, your phone, your admission ticket, any form of government-issued ID, any chargers you might need, and if applicable, any hardware you plan on using in your hack.

Can I get travel reimbursement?

We cannot provide a concrete answer to that question at the moment. Check back soon!

How long will I be at HackHSN?

HackHSN is exactly 12 hours from check-in to closing.

Some info on teams?

Teams can range anywhere from 1-4 people. If you do not have a team before the event, worry not - you can find one at the event or on our handy Slack server.

What can I make?

Anything!

How will hacks be evaluated?

Your projects will be evaluated based on their real-world application, technical complexity, and visual appeal, weighed in that order.

What are the prizes?

Prizes are TBA at the moment, but you can expect gift vouchers, software subscriptions, cool gadgets, and much more!

How do applications work?

Applications will be reviewed by the HackHSN board, and successful applicants will be notified via the email address on their application. West Windsor-Plainsboro students are guaranteed admittance.

What can I learn?

You will gain a tremendous amount of knowledge and experience simply by building your hack. There will also be fun workshops throughout the hackathon that you can optionally attend.
